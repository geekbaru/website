BANG KLANG

BANG KLANG

By-Shel Silverstein

I am Big Barney Zang of the railroad gang,
And my partner is Charlie 'O Flynn.
And I hold the nails for the big steel rails,
And Charlie, he hammers 'em in.
And most of the time, he does just fine,
but now and again, he fails,
Maybe tomorrow I'll hammer 'em in,
And let Charlie hold the nails.
